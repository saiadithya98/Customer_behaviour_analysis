# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis is a data analytics project that explores customer purchasing patterns using Python, PostgreSQL, and data visualization techniques. The project analyzes **3,900 customer purchase records** to uncover valuable business insights that help improve customer retention, marketing strategies, and revenue generation.

The analysis includes customer demographics, shopping behavior, subscription status, discounts, shipping preferences, and product performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify high-value customers and spending patterns.
* Evaluate the impact of discounts and subscriptions.
* Study shipping preferences and their effect on purchase value.
* Segment customers based on purchase history.
* Generate business recommendations using data-driven insights.

# 📈 Analysis Performed

### Revenue by Gender

Compared total revenue generated by male and female customers.

**Finding**

* Female customers generated slightly higher revenue than male customers.

---

### High-Value Discount Customers

Identified customers who:

* Spend above the average purchase amount
* Use available discounts

These customers represent smart shoppers with high purchasing power.

---

### Top Rated Products

Products receiving the highest customer satisfaction ratings include:

* Blouse ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
* Dress ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
* Shirt ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

### Shipping Preference Analysis

Compared purchase amounts across shipping methods.

**Finding**

* Express Shipping customers spend approximately **12% more** than Standard Shipping customers.

---

### Subscription Analysis

Studied the purchasing behavior of subscribed customers.

**Insights**

* Subscribers spend approximately **68% more**
* Subscribers contribute nearly **45% of total revenue**
* Higher customer loyalty among subscribers

---

### Customer Segmentation

Customers were classified into:

| Segment   | Description                 |
| --------- | --------------------------- |
| New       | First-time buyers           |
| Returning | Regular customers           |
| Loyal     | High-value repeat customers |

# 📊 Key Insights

* Female customers contribute slightly more revenue.
* Subscription customers generate higher revenue.
* Express shipping users spend more per transaction.
* Discount users can still be highly profitable.
* Top-rated products should receive greater marketing focus.
